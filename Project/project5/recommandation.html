My Top Anime Recommandations

Naruto

Naruto follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja ostracized by his village for hosting the Nine-Tails fox spirit. Driven by his dream to become Hokage (the village leader), Naruto trains relentlessly, forms bonds with allies like Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, and confronts adversaries while unraveling mysteries about his identity. The series spans his growth from a mischievous outcast to a hero protecting his world.

Attack on Titan

Humans survive in walled cities to escape giant humanoids known as Titans. Even Yeager, after witnessing the death of his mother in an attack by the Titans, joins the army to exterminate them. As the story unfolds, secrets emerge about the origins of the Titans and the true nature of the walls, which involve a great deal of testing of humanity and political irresistability.

Nezha: Birth of the Demon Child

A modern retelling of the Chinese myth of Nezha, a boy born from the destructive "Demon Orb" instead of the destined "Spirit Pearl." Branded a demon, Nezha fights societal prejudice and his prophesied fate with the support of his parents and mentor, Taiyi Zhenren. His bond with Ao Bing, the Dragon King’s son, and his ultimate sacrifice to save his town redefine heroism

Nezha 2: The Demon Tide

Following their near-death in the first film, Nezha and Ao Bing’s souls are preserved, but their bodies require reconstruction via the mystical Seven-Colored Lotus. The sequel escalates into a cosmic conflict as Nezha uncovers a celestial conspiracy led by Wuliang Xianweng, who frames dragons and demons to harvest immortality pills. Themes of institutional corruption and rebellion dominate as Nezha battles to save Chentang Pass and expose heavenly hypocrisy
